# Week 01 - Kitchen Setup + Knife Skills

## Focus

- Topic: Safe, efficient knife work and basic kitchen setup
- Core techniques: Grip, claw, basic cuts (dice, mince, chiffonade)
- Flavor focus: Clean, fresh flavors that highlight good prep

## Learning objectives

- Use a consistent chef's knife grip and claw technique without fatigue.
- Produce even cuts for predictable cook times.
- Build a simple pan sauce from fond after searing.

## Resources

- SFAH: Heat chapter (how heat affects cooking) + Salt chapter (seasoning basics)

## Mise en place and prep

- Sharpen and hone knives; check cutting board stability.
- Pre-wash and dry greens; prep a weeknight vinaigrette.
- Dice onions/carrots/celery for two meals; store in airtight container.
