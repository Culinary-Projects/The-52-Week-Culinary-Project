## Shopping Notes

**Week 11 pasta essentials:** "00" flour or all-purpose flour, large eggs (farm eggs if available — the yolks make a difference), whole-milk ricotta, fresh sage.

**Week 11 Monday tip:** Make a double batch of tomato sugo on Sunday — you'll use it Monday and the leftover becomes Pasta 2 on Saturday's dinner party if needed.

**Week 12 Tuesday:** Lamb shoulder needs 4 hours in the oven. Start it mid-afternoon or set it early and hold warm. Leftover lamb becomes the ragu for Wednesday's polenta.

**Week 12 Saturday:** Scallops must be completely dry before searing. Pat thoroughly on paper towels and let them sit uncovered on the rack in the fridge for 30 min before cooking. Wet scallops steam; dry scallops sear.
