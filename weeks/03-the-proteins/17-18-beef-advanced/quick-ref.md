## Shopping Notes

**Week 17:**
- **T-bone or porterhouse** — 1.5–2 inches thick; 1 per 1–2 people
- **Ribeye cap or bone-in ribeye** — ask your butcher specifically for the spinalis cap if available
- **Hanger steak** — 1–1.5 lbs; ask butcher to trim the tough center seam if possible
- **Flanken-cut short ribs (galbi)** — ½ inch thick, cross-cut; 2 lbs; Asian grocery stores often have these pre-cut

**Week 18:**
- **Ground beef** — 80/20, 1.5 lbs (Bolognese) — buy the cheapest 80/20 you can find
- **Beef short ribs (English cut)** — 3 lbs (Wednesday braise)
- **Flank steak** — 1.5–2 lbs (Friday)
- **Tamarind concentrate or paste** — small jar; Asian grocery stores; not tamarind candy
