# 🛒 Shopping List — Weeks 23–24: Shellfish

← [Back to Full Guide](index.md) | [Back to Quick Ref](quick-ref.md)

---

> **Shellfish mantra:** Buy it alive, cook it the day you buy it, and don't overthink anything.

---

## ⚠️ Equipment Check

- [ ] **Large Dutch oven or pot** — for mussels, clams, and bisque
- [ ] **Tongs + spider strainer** — for pulling shellfish out of liquid
- [ ] **Instant-read thermometer** — lobster tail target: 140°F
- [ ] **Sharp kitchen scissors** — for lobster shell if broiling
- [ ] **Shellfish brush** — for scrubbing mussels/clams

---

## 🦞 Proteins — Week 23

- [ ] **Manila or littleneck clams** — 2–3 dozen (Mon vongole)
- [ ] **Mussels** — 2 lbs (Tue moules marinière)
- [ ] **Live oysters** — 12 (Wed roasted)
- [ ] **Shrimp** — 1 lb, 16–20 count, peeled or shell-on (Fri scampi)
- [ ] **Live lobsters** — 2 × 1.25–1.5 lb (Sat project)

---

## 🦞 Proteins — Week 24

- [ ] **Blue crab or Dungeness crab** — 2 lbs (Mon chili crab); or frozen crab legs
- [ ] **Shrimp** — 1 lb, shell-on (Tue compound butter; save shells for stock)
- [ ] **Canned clams** — 3 × 6.5-oz cans (Wed chowder or clam pasta)
- [ ] **Mussels** — 2 lbs (Fri tomato-saffron)
- [ ] **Shellfish for plateau** — oysters (6), scallops (4 dry-packed), shrimp (½ lb) (Sat)

---

## 🥦 Produce

- [ ] Garlic — 3 heads (used every day)
- [ ] Shallots — 4
- [ ] Lemons — 8
- [ ] Fennel — 2 bulbs (Fri tomato-saffron broth + bisque aromatics)
- [ ] Fresh parsley — 2 bunches
- [ ] Cherry tomatoes — 1 pint (Tue Week 24)
- [ ] Canned San Marzano tomatoes — 1 can (Fri Week 24)
- [ ] Potatoes — 1 lb (chowder Week 24 Wed)
- [ ] Celery — 4 stalks (chowder aromatics)
- [ ] Heavy cream — 1 cup (Wed chowder)
- [ ] Corn — 2 ears or 1 cup frozen (if doing clam-corn pasta instead)

---

## 🧂 Pantry

- [ ] Dry white wine — 1 bottle (used Mon, Tue, and for bisque)
- [ ] Vermouth — can substitute for white wine in bisque
- [ ] Olive oil — extra-virgin
- [ ] Unsalted butter — ½ lb
- [ ] Spaghetti or linguine — 1 lb
- [ ] Saffron — pinch (Fri Week 24)
- [ ] Crushed red pepper flakes
- [ ] Oyster crackers or crusty baguette (Wed Week 24 chowder)
- [ ] Baguette — for both weeks (bread with broth is mandatory)
- [ ] Fish sauce or Worcestershire — small amount for bisque depth
- [ ] Sriracha and ketchup — for chili crab sauce (Mon Week 24)
- [ ] Eggs — 2 (chili crab sauce is egg-thickened)
- [ ] Jasmine rice — for Mon Week 24 (chili crab)
- [ ] Cornichons, shallot vinegar — for oyster mignonette (Sat Week 24)
