# Weeks 45–46: Desserts — Shopping List

← [Weeks 43–44: Pastry](../43-44-pastry/index.md) | [Weeks 47–48: Signature Dishes →](../47-48-signature-dishes/index.md)

---

## Equipment Check

- [ ] Kitchen torch (essential for crème brûlée brûlée step)
- [ ] 4–6 ramekins (crème brûlée / panna cotta)
- [ ] Stand or hand mixer (pavlova meringue, buttercream frosting)
- [ ] 9-inch round cake pans × 3 (layer cake)
- [ ] Bench scraper (frosting the cake)
- [ ] Pastry brush (simple syrup on cake layers)
- [ ] Candy/instant-read thermometer (caramel, cookie dough, custard)
- [ ] Large roasting pan (bain-marie for crème brûlée)
- [ ] Parchment paper
- [ ] Springform pan (optional — tiramisu, mousse presentation)

---

## Dairy & Eggs

- [ ] Heavy cream — 2 quarts
- [ ] Whole milk — 1 quart
- [ ] Mascarpone — 8 oz (tiramisu)
- [ ] Unsalted butter — 2 lbs (layer cake, caramel, cookies, sticky toffee)
- [ ] Eggs — 3 dozen (custards, mousse, meringue, cake, cookies)
- [ ] Crème fraîche or sour cream — 1 cup (optional: fold into whipped cream for pavlova)
- [ ] Vanilla ice cream — 1 pint (sticky toffee pudding accompaniment)

---

## Chocolate

- [ ] Bittersweet chocolate (70%) — 12 oz (mousse + truffles)
- [ ] Semi-sweet or milk chocolate chips — 12 oz (chocolate chip cookies)
- [ ] Dutch-process cocoa powder — ¼ cup (truffle coating, cake option)

---

## Pantry & Baking

- [ ] Granulated sugar — 5 lbs
- [ ] Brown sugar (dark) — 2 lbs (cookies, sticky toffee, butterscotch)
- [ ] Powdered/confectioners' sugar — 1 lb (buttercream frosting)
- [ ] All-purpose flour — 5 lbs
- [ ] Bread flour — 2 lbs (chocolate chip cookies — more chew)
- [ ] Cake flour — 2 lbs (layer cake — finer crumb)
- [ ] Baking powder — 1 tin
- [ ] Baking soda — 1 box
- [ ] Cornstarch — 1 box (pavlova, pastry cream)
- [ ] Salt (Diamond Crystal kosher) — 1 box
- [ ] Flaky sea salt (Maldon) — finishing for cookies
- [ ] Powdered gelatin — 2 packets (panna cotta; possibly mousse)
- [ ] Vanilla extract — 1 bottle (or vanilla bean paste)
- [ ] Vanilla beans — 2 pods (crème brûlée + panna cotta)
- [ ] Espresso or strong coffee — 1 cup brewed (tiramisu)
- [ ] Coffee liqueur (Kahlúa or Tia Maria) — optional for tiramisu
- [ ] Dates (Medjool) — 8 oz (sticky toffee pudding)
- [ ] Ladyfingers (Savoiardi) — 1 package (tiramisu)
- [ ] White vinegar or cream of tartar — (pavlova stability)
- [ ] Chocolate-covered espresso beans — optional garnish

---

## Fruit & Produce

- [ ] Lemons — 4 (pavlova topping; cake option)
- [ ] Kiwi — 4–6 (pavlova)
- [ ] Passion fruit — 4 (pavlova topping; or top with strawberries)
- [ ] Strawberries — 1 pint (pavlova and/or frosting between layers)
- [ ] Raspberries or raspberry jam — 1 cup (panna cotta coulis + layer cake option)

---

## Layer Cake Supplies

- [ ] Cake strips (wet fabric strips for pans) — or DIY with damp towel strips
- [ ] Cake board or large plate for assembly
- [ ] Offset spatula or large spoon for frosting
- [ ] Cocktail stick / cake tester (done test)

---

## Planning Notes

**Make-ahead order for Week 45:**
1. **Sat/Sun:** Review recipes; buy all supplies
2. **Mon:** Crème brûlée (make in morning, refrigerate all day; brûlée before serving)
3. **Tue:** Panna cotta (make morning; unmold at night — 6h minimum)
4. **Wed:** Chocolate mousse (make morning; chill 4h minimum)
5. **Fri:** Pavlova (bake morning; top immediately before serving)
6. **Sat:** Tiramisu (make morning × 4h; serve in evening)

**Make-ahead order for Week 46:**
1. **Mon:** Caramel sauce (keeps refrigerated 2 weeks)
2. **Tue:** Sticky toffee pudding (bake; pour sauce over; reheat portions as needed)
3. **Wed:** Cookie dough (make; refrigerate overnight; bake Thu morning or Fri)
4. **Fri:** Layer cake (bake layers Thu if needed; assemble Friday; refrigerate overnight)
5. **Sat:** Dessert party — pull from fridge; arrange; serve

**Note:** Crème brûlée sugar must be brûléed immediately before serving — never ahead.
