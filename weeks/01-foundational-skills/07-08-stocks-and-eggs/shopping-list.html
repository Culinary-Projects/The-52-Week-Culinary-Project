    🛒 Shopping List — Weeks 7–8: Stocks & Eggs

    ← Back to Full Guide | Back to Quick Ref

    How to use this list: Week 7 is about using your stock. Get the Week 2 chicken carcass out of the freezer on Sunday night to thaw. Week 8 is egg week — buy 3 dozen eggs. You will use all of them.

    ⚠️ Pre-Week Check

    • [ ] Week 2 chicken carcass from the freezer — this is your Week 7 Monday stock. If you didn't save it, buy a raw chicken carcass from your butcher or supermarket.
    • [ ] Week 7 Saturday: Buy beef bones (knuckle bones and marrow bones) for beef stock — order ahead or request from your butcher. You need 3–4 lbs.

    🥩 Proteins — Week 7

    • [ ] Chicken pieces (thighs + drumsticks) — 4 pieces (Week 7 Tue pan sauce)
    • [ ] Mixed seafood for bouillabaisse: shrimp (½ lb), mussels (1 lb), clams (1 lb), cod or halibut chunks (1 lb), or 1 lb monkfish — (Week 7 Wed)
    • [ ] Shrimp shells (or save from above) — freeze for quick shrimp stock or fumet base
    • [ ] Beef knuckle and/or marrow bones — 3–4 lbs (Week 7 Sat beef stock, all day project)

    🥩 Proteins — Week 8

    • [ ] Eggs — 3 dozen (every method this week: scrambles, poaches, omelettes, carbonara, deviled, shakshuka, crème brûlée)
    • [ ] Hot-smoked salmon or lox — 4 oz (Week 8 Mon)
    • [ ] Thick-cut bacon — 6 oz (Week 8 Tue + Week 8 Sat deviled eggs garnish)
    • [ ] Guanciale or pancetta (thick pieces) — 4 oz (Week 8 Fri carbonara)
    • [ ] Whole milk or heavy cream — 1 pint (Week 8 Sat crème brûlée — use full-fat cream)

    🥦 Produce — Week 7

    • [ ] Yellow onions — 6 large (Week 7 Sat French onion soup + stock mirepoix)
    • [ ] Carrots — 6 (stock vegetables + week meals)
    • [ ] Celery — 1 bunch (stock vegetables)
    • [ ] Fennel — 1 bulb (bouillabaisse + stock)
    • [ ] Leeks — 2 (vegetable stock, Week 7 Fri)
    • [ ] Mushrooms (cremini or mixed) — 1 lb (Week 7 Fri risotto)
    • [ ] Arugula — 2 bags (Week 7 Fri salad + Week 8 Mon salad)
    • [ ] Bitter greens (radicchio, endive) — 1 head each (Week 7 Tue salad)
    • [ ] Garlic — 2 heads
    • [ ] Fresh thyme, parsley, bay leaves — 1 bunch each (stocks)
    • [ ] Tomatoes or canned tomatoes — 2 cans (bouillabaisse)
    • [ ] Saffron — 1 small tin (bouillabaisse — expensive but essential)

    🥦 Produce — Week 8

    • [ ] Baby spinach or Swiss chard — 1 bag (Week 8 Tue poached eggs)
    • [ ] Roma tomatoes — 4 (Week 8 Tue oven roasted)
    • [ ] Fresh chives — 1 bunch (Week 8 Mon scrambled eggs + omelette)
    • [ ] Fresh tarragon — 1 small bunch (omelette filling, optional)
    • [ ] Sourdough bread — 1 loaf (Week 8 Mon toast + omelette bread)
    • [ ] Baguette or crusty bread — 1 (Week 8 Wed)
    • [ ] Canned tomatoes — 2 cans (Week 8 Sat shakshuka)
    • [ ] Red bell pepper — 2 (shakshuka)
    • [ ] Yellow onion — 2 (shakshuka + French onion soup Sat if making again)

    🧀 Dairy

    • [ ] Unsalted butter — 4 sticks (Week 8 omelettes use a lot; Week 7 risotto finishing)
    • [ ] Crème fraîche or sour cream — 1 tub (Week 8 Mon with smoked salmon)
    • [ ] Parmesan (block) — 8 oz (Week 7 risotto + Week 8 carbonara)
    • [ ] Pecorino Romano — 4 oz (Week 8 Fri carbonara — use together with parmesan)
    • [ ] Heavy cream — 1 pint (Week 8 Sat crème brûlée needs 2 cups)
    • [ ] Gruyère — 6 oz (Week 7 Sat French onion soup topping)

    🌾 Pantry & Dry Goods

    • [ ] Arborio or Carnaroli rice — 1.5 cups (Week 7 Fri risotto)
    • [ ] Ramen noodles — 4 servings (Week 7 Mon)
    • [ ] Spaghetti or rigatoni — 1 lb (Week 8 Fri carbonara)
    • [ ] Dry white wine — 1 bottle (risotto + pan sauces)
    • [ ] Red wine — 1 bottle (Week 7 Sat French onion soup braising base)
    • [ ] Beef stock — 64 oz (Week 7 Tue pan sauce + Week 7 Sat French onion soup)
    • [ ] Chicken stock — 64 oz (risotto + ramen base)
    • [ ] Soy sauce — 1 bottle (ramen seasoning)
    • [ ] Mirin — 1 bottle (ramen tare)
    • [ ] Kombu — a few strips (ramen base — found at Asian grocery stores or Amazon)
    • [ ] White wine vinegar (ramen seasoning, risotto touch)
    • [ ] Granulated sugar — 1 cup (Week 8 Sat crème brûlée)
    • [ ] Vanilla beans — 2 (crème brûlée — or 1 tsp pure vanilla extract)
    • [ ] Mayo, Dijon, paprika, capers — small amounts (Week 8 Sat deviled eggs)
    • [ ] Cumin, smoked paprika, chili flakes — (Week 8 Sat shakshuka)
    • [ ] Rouille ingredients: jarred roasted red peppers, garlic, breadcrumbs (bouillabaisse)

    🥖 Bread

    • [ ] Baguette — 2 loaves (Week 7 Sat French onion soup + general use)
    • [ ] Thick-sliced sourdough — 1 loaf (Week 8 Mon + Wed)

    🔧 Equipment (Nice to Have for This Block)

    • [ ] Kitchen torch — for crème brûlée (Week 8 Sat). Can substitute: broiler works, but you have less control. A cheap culinary torch costs less than $20.
    • [ ] Fine mesh strainer (chinois) — essential for clarifying stocks. If you don't have one, a colander lined with cheesecloth works.
    • [ ] Stockpot (8+ quart) — if you're making proper beef stock on Week 7 Saturday.

    📝 Notes

    Beef stock (Week 7 Sat) is an all-day project. Roast the bones at 450°F for 45 minutes first (this is what makes beef stock different from chicken stock). Then 6–8 hours of simmering. Start by 10 AM.

    Carbonara (Week 8 Fri) — no cream. Real carbonara is eggs, cheese, pasta water, and guanciale. The heat from the pasta cooks the eggs into a silky custard. Take the pan fully off the heat before adding the egg mixture. Temperature is everything.

    Crème brûlée (Week 8 Sat) — make it the day before. They need 4–6 hours to set in the fridge after baking. Brûlée the tops right before serving.
