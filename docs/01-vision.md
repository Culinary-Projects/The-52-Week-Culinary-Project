# Vision

## Goal

Build a modern, skill-progressive culinary curriculum that maps daily dinners to weekly technique themes.

## Success criteria

- Every dinner aligns with the weekly topic or a supporting sub-skill.
- Weeknights stay in the 30-45 minute window.
- Each week includes one major Saturday meal that integrates the core techniques.
- Learning materials accompany each week (SFAH, Serious Eats, and other reputable sources).

## Constraints

- Keep the plan practical for home cooking.
- Focus on techniques, not only recipes.
- Favor recipes that teach transferable skills.
- Maintain variety across proteins, cuisines, and cooking methods.
