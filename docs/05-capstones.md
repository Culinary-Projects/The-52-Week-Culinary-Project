# Capstones Framework

This is intentionally flexible. Add capstones as you define the cadence (monthly, every 6 weeks, or by skill cluster).

## Capstone types

- Technique check: demonstrate a specific technique under time.
- Menu build: 2-3 course meal with timing plan.
- Skill integration: combine two or more weekly skills into one dinner.
- Ingredient focus: build a meal around one ingredient in three forms.
- Service simulation: plan, cook, plate, and serve on a clock.

## Suggested cadence (optional)

- Skill cluster capstone every 4-6 weeks.
- Bigger capstone at weeks 26 and 52.
