# Schedule Rules

Use these rules to keep the plan realistic and consistent.

## Time windows

- Weeknights: 30-45 minutes total.
- Saturday: 60-180 minutes for the major meal.
- Sunday: 15-30 minutes for prep or a light drill.

## Alignment rules

- Every dinner aligns with the weekly topic or a sub-skill.
- Use the Saturday meal to integrate 2-3 core techniques.
- Weeknight meals should reinforce one technique each.

## Workload balance

- Aim for 3 protein-based dinners and 2 veg/legume-based dinners each week.
- Reuse ingredients across the week to reduce waste.
- Add one low-effort dinner if the week includes a complex Saturday meal.
