# ⏰ Service 55 — Afternoon Tea

[← Block 47–48: Pastry — Doughs & Custards Overview](index.md)

---

**Skill:** Multi-item pastry production; timing and staging; composed sweet/savory spread
**Goal:** Produce a proper afternoon tea service using everything from Blocks 47–48. This is a project service that tests your ability to produce multiple pastry items in a coordinated window.

**Required elements:**
- Scones (basic cream scones with clotted cream and jam)
- Finger sandwiches (cucumber + cream cheese; smoked salmon)
- One sweet pastry from this week (lemon tart, éclairs, or mille-feuille)
- Tea, brewed properly

**Full Meal:**
- The afternoon tea IS the meal — invite someone to share it

---

> 🎥 **Compare Notes:** [Afternoon Tea — Traditional British Recipes — My Fancy Baking](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=c0GfXs57S9I) — Full afternoon tea spread including scones, finger sandwiches, and petit fours with presentation tips.
