# ⏰ Service 53 — Fermentation Tasting

[← Block 41–42: Fermentation & Preservation Overview](index.md)

---

**Skill:** Evaluating fermentation stages; comparing fermented vs. vinegar-pickled flavors
**Goal:** Line up your sauerkraut (at whatever stage it's reached), your quick pickles, your hot sauce, and your gravlax. Taste each alongside a store-bought equivalent. The fermented products should have more depth, more sourness, and a "living" quality that preserved-in-vinegar products lack.

**Full Meal:**
- Fermentation tasting board: sauerkraut, pickled vegetables, hot sauce, gravlax
- Crusty bread, mustard, crème fraîche, and whatever cheese pairs well
- This is a celebration of patience
