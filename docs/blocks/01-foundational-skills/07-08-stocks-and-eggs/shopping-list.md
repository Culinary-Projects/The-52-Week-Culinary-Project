## 📝 Notes

**Beef stock (Week 7 Sat) is an all-day project.** Roast the bones at 450°F for 45 minutes first (this is what makes beef stock different from chicken stock). Then 6–8 hours of simmering. Start by 10 AM.

**Carbonara (Week 8 Fri) — no cream.** Real carbonara is eggs, cheese, pasta water, and guanciale. The heat from the pasta cooks the eggs into a silky custard. Take the pan fully off the heat before adding the egg mixture. Temperature is everything.

**Crème brûlée (Week 8 Sat) — make it the day before.** They need 4–6 hours to set in the fridge after baking. Brûlée the tops right before serving.
