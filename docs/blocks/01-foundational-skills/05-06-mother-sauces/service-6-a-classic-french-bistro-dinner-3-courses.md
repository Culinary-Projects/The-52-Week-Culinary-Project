# ⏰ Service 6 — A Classic French Bistro Dinner (3 Courses)

[← Block 5–6: The 5 Mother Sauces Overview](index.md)

---

**Skill:** The capstone for two weeks of mother sauces. You'll make a béarnaise (hollandaise's most famous daughter sauce), a Mornay gratin (béchamel from Block 5, from memory), and a vinaigrette without looking at a recipe. Three sauces from two mother-sauce families, all on the table at the same time.

| Course | Dish | Sauce | Link |
|--------|------|-------|------|
| **Salad** | Lyonnaise Salad (Frisée aux Lardons) | Vinaigrette (from memory) | [Recipe](https://www.seriouseats.com/spring-salad-with-poached-eggs-and-bread-crumbs) |
| **Main** | Pan-Seared Steak with Béarnaise | Béarnaise (hollandaise daughter) | [Steak](https://www.bbcgoodfood.com/recipes/steak-chips) · [Béarnaise](https://www.seriouseats.com/foolproof-bearnaise-sauce-recipe) |
| **Side** | Cauliflower Gratin with Mornay | Mornay / Béchamel (Block 5 recall) | [Recipe](https://www.bbcgoodfood.com/recipes/cauliflower-cheese) |

| Component | Recipe |
|-----------|--------|
| Protein | Pan-seared steak (ribeye or NY strip) |
| Sauce | Béarnaise — tarragon-shallot reduction + hollandaise emulsion |
| Side | Cauliflower gratin — béchamel enriched with Gruyère (Mornay), baked until golden |
| Salad | Frisée aux lardons with warm Dijon vinaigrette |

*Skill targets: Béarnaise that holds. Béchamel/Mornay from memory. Vinaigrette without a recipe. Everything plated together.*

---

> 🎥 **Compare Notes:** [Grandma's Favorite Steak — Jacques Pépin Cooking At Home (KQED)](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=5pEO78nIOwQ) — Watch how casually Pépin handles the steak and the sauce. That confidence with heat and timing is what two weeks of sauce work has been building toward.
