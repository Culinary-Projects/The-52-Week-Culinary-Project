## 📝 Notes

**Moussaka (Week 5 Sat) — the day-before prep:** Slice eggplant, salt generously, and let drain on paper towels for 30 minutes. This removes bitterness and prevents a watery moussaka. Béchamel also benefits from being made fresh.

**Lobster rolls (Week 6 Tue):** Pre-cooked whole lobster from a fishmonger is easiest. Or buy frozen lobster tails and steam them. Large shrimp are a fine substitute if lobster is cost-prohibitive.

**Steak frites (Week 6 Sat):** Frites need a double-fry (once at 325°F to cook through, once at 375°F to crisp). Plan to start them 30 minutes before the steaks. Or make oven fries at 450°F — not quite the same but much less mess.
