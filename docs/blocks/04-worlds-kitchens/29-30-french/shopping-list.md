# 🛒 Shopping List — Block 29–30: French Classics

← [Back to Full Guide](index.md) | [Back to Quick Ref](quick-ref.md)

---

> **Plan ahead:** Cassoulet (Block 27) requires dried whites beans soaked overnight. Duck confit (Block 28) is better if started a day ahead. Plan accordingly.

---

## ⚠️ Equipment Check

- [ ] **Large Dutch oven / cocotte** — essential for cassoulet
- [ ] **Blender** — for foolproof hollandaise (Block 27)
- [ ] **Soufflé dish (6-cup)** — for cheese soufflé (Block 28)
- [ ] **Fine-mesh strainer** — for sauces
- [ ] **Heat-proof bowl** — beurre blanc and béarnaise work

---

## 🥩 Proteins — Block 27

- [ ] **Duck confit** — store-bought legs × 4 (cassoulet; or make your own from Block 14 technique)
- [ ] **Toulouse sausage or garlic sausage** — 1 lb (cassoulet)
- [ ] **Salt pork or pork belly** — 8 oz (cassoulet)
- [ ] **Steak** — strip or sirloin × 2 (béarnaise + frites)
- [ ] **Canadian bacon** — 4 slices (eggs benedict)
- [ ] **Bacon lardons** — 4 oz (bistro salad Block 28)

---

## 🥩 Proteins — Block 28

- [ ] **Halibut or salmon** — 2 fillets (beurre blanc)
- [ ] **Whole duck, 4–5 lb** OR **2 duck breasts** (à l'orange)
- [ ] **Salt cod, if available** — 8 oz for grand aioli (Block 28; optional — other protein works)
- [ ] **Eggs** — 2 dozen (hollandaise, poached eggs, soufflé, aioli, salad Lyonnaise)

---

## 🥦 Produce — Block 27

- [ ] Russet or Yukon gold potatoes — 2 lbs (steak frites — separate from aioli platter potatoes)
- [ ] Onions — 5 lbs (French onion soup — ~3 lbs per pot)
- [ ] Garlic — 3 heads
- [ ] Shallots — 6
- [ ] Fresh thyme — 2 bunches
- [ ] Bay leaves
- [ ] Gruyère cheese — 6 oz (gratinée + soufflé)
- [ ] Lemons — 6
- [ ] Dried cannellini or Great Northern beans — 1 lb (cassoulet — soak night)
- [ ] Canned tomatoes — 1 can (cassoulet)

---

## 🥦 Produce — Block 28

- [ ] Oranges — 4 (duck à l'orange: juice + zest)
- [ ] Asparagus — 1 bunch (beurre blanc)
- [ ] Frisée lettuce — 2 heads (salad Lyonnaise)
- [ ] Assorted vegetables for grand aioli : carrots, green beans, radishes, cauliflower, beets, potatoes
- [ ] Garlic — 1 additional head (aioli)
- [ ] Cherry tomatoes — 1 pint (aioli platter)
- [ ] Fresh tarragon — 1 bunch (béarnaise + various)

---

## 🧂 Pantry

- [ ] Beef or chicken stock — 2 quarts (onion soup)
- [ ] Cognac or brandy — small bottle (cassoulet + various)
- [ ] Dry white wine — 2 bottles (beurre blanc, mussels, soufflé)
- [ ] Dry red wine — 1 bottle (cassoulet)
- [ ] White wine vinegar (béarnaise reduction)
- [ ] Dijon mustard — large jar (vinaigrettes + various)
- [ ] Unsalted butter — 2 lbs (beurre blanc eats butter; hollandaise, soufflé)
- [ ] Heavy cream — 1 pint (soufflé béchamel + various)
- [ ] Parmesan — 4 oz (soufflé)
- [ ] Baguette — multiple (soufflé croutons, onion soup croutons, aioli platter)
- [ ] English muffins — 1 pack (Block 27 eggs benedict)
- [ ] Dark chocolate (70%+) — 4 oz (chocolate mousse — classic French bistro dessert)
- [ ] Olive oil — extra-virgin (aioli + dressings)
- [ ] Duck fat — small jar if making duck confit vs. buying
- [ ] Orange marmalade — small jar (à l'orange backup if juice not enough)
| Whole black peppercorns — for gastrique
