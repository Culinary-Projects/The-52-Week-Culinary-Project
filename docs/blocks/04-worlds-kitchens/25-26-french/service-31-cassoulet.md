# ⏰ Service 31 — Cassoulet

---

**Skill:** Bean preparation; regional French braising (multi-component slow dish)  
**Read first:** [Classic Cassoulet — Bon Appétit](https://www.bonappetit.com/recipe/classic-cassoulet)
**SE reference:** [Traditional French Cassoulet — SE](https://www.seriouseats.com/traditional-french-cassoulet-recipe)

**What you're learning:** Cassoulet is the great peasant dish of southwestern France: confit duck, pork sausage, white beans, and a crust that forms and gets pushed down repeatedly. It's a multi-component dish requiring 4–6 hours during the project session. The payoff is extraordinary. Start by noon on project day.

**Full Meal:**
- Cassoulet (serves 4–6; freeze the rest)
- Warm sourdough — sliced thick, toasted
- Frisée Salad with Walnut Vinaigrette and Shaved Gruyère

---

> 🎥 **Compare Notes:** [Cassoulet de Toulouse — Food Wishes](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=uOL-IgFC61c) — Chef John's Toulouse-style cassoulet; compare your layering technique, crust development, and bean texture.
