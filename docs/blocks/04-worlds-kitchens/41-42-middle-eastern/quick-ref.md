## Middle Eastern Pantry Essentials

| Ingredient | Role | Source |
|------------|------|--------|
| [Tahini](https://www.seriouseats.com/hummus-b-tahini-hummus-tahini-recipe-11887965) | Sesame paste; base for hummus, sauces | Soom or Seed + Mill brand |
| Sumac | Tart red spice; dressings, kebab finish | Middle Eastern market |
| Za'atar | Thyme-based herb blend; used on bread and labneh | Middle Eastern market |
| Pomegranate molasses | [Tart-sweet syrup; fattoush + glazes](https://www.seriouseats.com/fattoush-recipe-8638102) | Middle Eastern market |
| Dried black lime (loomi) | Intensely citrusy; used whole in stews | Middle Eastern market |
| Rose water | Floral; desserts + kabsa | Middle Eastern market |
| Harissa | [North African chile paste](https://www.seriouseats.com/shakshuka-north-african-shirred-eggs-tomato-pepper-recipe) | Available widely |
| Labneh | Strained yogurt; smooth, tart | Make by straining Greek yogurt overnight |

## Key Ratios

| Preparation | Ratio |
|-------------|-------|
| [Hummus tahini](https://www.seriouseats.com/hummus-b-tahini-hummus-tahini-recipe-11887965) | [1 cup cooked chickpeas : ¼ cup tahini : 2 tbsp lemon : 1 garlic clove](https://www.seriouseats.com/israeli-style-tahini-sauce-recipe) |
| [Tabbouleh](https://www.seriouseats.com/tabbouleh-salad-recipe) | By volume: ¾ parsley : ¼ everything else (not the reverse) |
| [Tahini sauce](https://www.seriouseats.com/israeli-style-tahini-sauce-recipe) | [1 part tahini : 1 part lemon juice : garlic + water to thin](https://www.seriouseats.com/israeli-style-tahini-sauce-recipe) |
| [Falafel](https://www.seriouseats.com/the-food-lab-vegan-experience-best-homemade-falafel-recipe) | Soak dried chickpeas 12–24 hrs; do not cook; grind raw |
