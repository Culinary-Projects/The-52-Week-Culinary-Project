# Module 3: The Proteins (Blocks 17–30)


---

> **The goal of this module:** Master every major protein category — lamb and game, beef from cheap cuts to prime, pork in all its forms, finfish and shellfish, and finally the preserved meats of charcuterie. Each two-week block adds a new protein to your permanent repertoire.

By the end of Week 30, you should be able to handle any cut of any animal with confidence and intention.

## Core Skills Covered

- **Lamb & game** — rack, shoulder, shanks; braising and roasting; duck confit; temperature and timing for pink lamb
- **Beef** — steaks (sear, reverse sear, pan sauce); braising (short ribs, chuck, beef cheeks); ground beef and burgers; beef stock
- **Fish** — filleting round and flat fish; skin-on sear; en papillote; ceviche acid-cook; freshness indicators; whole fish grilling; curing and gravlax
- **Shellfish** — live and frozen; steaming, grilling, bisque; breaking down a lobster or Dungeness crab
- **Pork** — pork belly, shoulder, loin, and ribs; cure ratios, brining, fat rendering, crackling
- **Charcuterie** — dry-curing, equilibrium salt method, pork rillettes, pâté, terrines; food safety in curing
