# 🛒 Shopping List — Block 21–22: Beef Advanced Cuts

← [Back to Full Guide](index.md) | [Back to Quick Ref](quick-ref.md)

---

> **How to use this list:** Block 17 is grilling week — set up your outdoor setup before . Block 18 has a long braise on (start by 2:30 PM). The galbi (Block 17) needs overnight marinade — buy and prep on . The stuffed flank steak (Block 18) benefits from butterflying ahead of time.

---

## ⚠️ Equipment Check

- [ ] **Outdoor grill or grill pan** — gas or charcoal; need two-zone setup capability
- [ ] **Charcoal chimney** — if using charcoal; skip lighter fluid
- [ ] **Instant-read thermometer** — still essential
- [ ] **Kitchen twine** — for tying the stuffed flank steak (Block 18)

---

## 🥩 Proteins — Block 17

- [ ] **T-bone or porterhouse** — 1.5–2 inch thick; 1 per person 
- [ ] **Ribeye cap (spinalis)** OR **bone-in ribeye** — call your butcher ahead 
- [ ] **Hanger steak** — 1–1.5 lbs; ask butcher to trim center seam if possible 
- [ ] **Flanken-cut short ribs (galbi)** — ½ inch cross-cut; 2 lbs. Asian grocery stores often stock these; standard grocery stores may not (marinate night)

---

## 🥩 Proteins — Block 18

- [ ] **Ground beef** — 80/20; 1.5 lbs (Bolognese)
- [ ] **Strip steak or hanger** — for steak salad; leftovers from Block 17 also work
- [ ] **Beef short ribs** — English cut; 3 lbs (tamarind braise)
- [ ] **Flank steak** — 1.5–2 lbs (stuffed flank; ask butcher to butterfly if you prefer)
