Cook 4–5 vegetable preparations using different techniques. Serve them as a tasting — small plates, one at a time, with bread.

| Course | Dish | Technique |
|--------|------|-----------|
| 1 | [Roasted Root Purée](https://www.seriouseats.com/rich-nutty-roasted-cauliflower-puree-recipe) with roasted garlic oil | Purée |
| 2 | Blanched asparagus with [brown butter](https://www.seriouseats.com/how-to-make-brown-butter-technique) and fried capers | Blanching + beurre noisette |
| 3 | Charred cabbage with miso-sesame | Charring |
| 4 | [Sautéed mushrooms](https://www.seriouseats.com/sauteed-mushrooms-recipe-7972096) on toast | Sear + braise |
| 5 | [Quick-pickled vegetables](https://www.seriouseats.com/pickled-red-onions) + bread | Fermentation + pickling |
