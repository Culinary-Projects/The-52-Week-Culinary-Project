## Pastry Fat Reference

| Dough | Fat | Amount (vs. flour) | Result |
|-------|-----|-------------------|--------|
| [Pâte sucrée](https://www.seriouseats.com/pate-sucree-tart-shell-recipe) | Unsalted butter | ~50–60% | Crisp, cookie-like, not flaky |
| Pâte brisée | Unsalted butter | ~40% | Flaky-tender |
| American pie crust | Butter + lard | ~60% combined | Flaky, rich |
| Rough puff | European butter | ~75% | Flaky, layered |
| Brioche | Unsalted butter | 50–60% | Rich, tender, not pastry-crisp |
| [Choux](https://www.seriouseats.com/choux-au-craquelin) | Unsalted butter | 40% of water weight | Hollow, crisped shell |

## Custard Ratio Reference

| Preparation | Eggs | Liquid | Temp |
|-------------|------|--------|------|
| [Pastry cream](https://www.seriouseats.com/vanilla-pastry-cream-creme-patissiere) | 4 yolks per 500ml milk | Milk + cornstarch | Cook to 200°F (thick and glossy) |
| [Quiche custard](https://www.seriouseats.com/quiche-lorraine-recipe-8715255) | 1 egg per ¼ cup cream | Heavy cream | Pull at 160°F |
| Lemon curd | 4 yolks + 2 whole | Lemon juice + butter | Cook to 170°F |
| Crème brûlée | 5–6 yolks per 500ml | [Heavy cream + vanilla](https://www.seriouseats.com/vanilla-pastry-cream-creme-patissiere) | Bake to 160°F |
