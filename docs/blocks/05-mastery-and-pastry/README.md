# Module 5: Mastery & Pastry (Blocks 53–62)


---

> **The goal of this module:** Finish strong. Bread and pastry demand precision that sharpens every other skill you've built. Then design your own signature dishes, learn to orchestrate a full dinner party, and cap the year with a self-administered final exam that proves — to yourself — that you can cook.

By the end of Week 62, you will have completed a full professional-level culinary curriculum, delivered at home, over one year.

## Core Skills Covered

- **Bread** — levain maintenance; autolyse, bulk ferment, shaping, scoring; Pullman loaf for beginners; baking in Dutch oven
- **Pastry** — pâte brisée, pâte sucrée, rough puff; lamination logic; blind-baking; tart construction; choux
- **Desserts** — crème brûlée; layer cake; Swiss and Italian meringue; ganache; bain-marie technique; tempering chocolate
- **Signature dishes** — developing, documenting, and refining 3 original dishes that reflect your palate and history
- **Menus & timing** — backwards-planning a dinner party; mise en place at scale; timing charts; expediting
- **Final exam** — multi-course meal planned, shopped, and executed solo; written reflection on the year
