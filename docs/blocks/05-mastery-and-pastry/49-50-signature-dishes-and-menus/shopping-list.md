## Notes for Planning

**Protein math:**
- 6 oz raw per person for a boneless centerpiece protein
- 8–10 oz raw per person for bone-in (bone adds weight; guests eat less net)
- Braises typically lose 25–30% weight during cooking — buy more than it seems you need

**Alcohol-free dinner party pairings to source:**
- Sparkling water (multiple bottles — this runs out)
- Citrus — lemon, lime for sparkling water or shrub drinks
- Shrub or drinking vinegar: look at specialty food stores or make your own
- Good-quality non-alcoholic sparkling wine if available

**For guests with dietary restrictions:**
- Ask at invitation time, not the day before
- Plan one substitution path for each restriction rather than an entirely separate meal
- Most vegetarian adaptations of your protein dish work well (roasted mushrooms, cauliflower steak, or a well-seasoned legume dish at the centerpiece)
