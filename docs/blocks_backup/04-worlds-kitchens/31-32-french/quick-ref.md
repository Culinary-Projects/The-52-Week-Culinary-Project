## Key French Sauce Reference

| Sauce | Base | Emulsifier | Served With |
|-------|------|------------|-------------|
| [Hollandaise](https://www.seriouseats.com/foolproof-2-minute-hollandaise-recipe) | Clarified butter | Egg yolks + lemon | Eggs, asparagus, fish |
| [Béarnaise](https://www.seriouseats.com/foolproof-bearnaise-sauce-recipe) | Clarified butter | Egg yolks + tarragon reduction | Steak, lamb |
| [Beurre Blanc](https://www.seriouseats.com/beurre-blanc-recipe-7643412) | Whole butter | Shallot + white wine reduction | Fish, scallops, vegetables |
| [Aioli](https://www.seriouseats.com/two-minute-foolproof-aioli-recipe) | Olive oil | Egg yolk + garlic | Vegetables, fish, bread |
| Gastrique | Sugar + acid | n/a — syrup, not emulsion | Duck, game, rich meats |

## Cassoulet Component Timing (project session)

| Component | Cook Time | Notes |
|-----------|-----------|-------|
| Soak white beans | Overnight or quick-soak | Cannellini or Great Northern |
| Duck confit | If not already made: 2 hrs | Can use store-bought duck confit |
| Toulouse sausage or garlic sausage | 20 min | Prick first |
| Salt pork or pork belly | 1 hr simmer | Blanch first to remove excess salt |
| [Full cassoulet assembly](https://www.seriouseats.com/traditional-french-cassoulet-recipe) | 3–4 hrs oven | [Break crust 3–4× during cooking](https://www.seriouseats.com/guide-to-cooking-with-wine-11921603) |
