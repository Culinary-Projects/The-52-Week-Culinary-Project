## Key Reminders

- **Before every session:** Hone the knife. Pat proteins completely dry.
- **Don't crowd the pan.** One layer, high heat, batches if needed.
- **Friday is a freeform cook** — close the recipes, use the techniques.
- **Saturday short ribs:** 3–4 hours. Start early. Internal temp 200°F+ for full collagen breakdown.
