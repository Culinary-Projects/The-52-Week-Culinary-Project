# 🛒 Shopping List — Weeks 1–2: Knife Skills & Kitchen Setup

← [Back to Full Guide](index.md) | [Back to Quick Ref](quick-ref.md)

---

> **How to use this list:** Do this in two produce trips — one before Week 1 starts, one before Week 2 starts. The list is already split that way. Produce bought in one go at the start of Week 1 will not survive to Week 2.
>
> **Pantry and dry goods** (pasta, sauces, oils, spices) can all be bought in one trip before Week 1 — they keep indefinitely.
>
> **Proteins:** Buy the Week 1 proteins (chicken breasts, shrimp, beef) before Week 1. Buy the Week 2 Pad Thai protein (shrimp or tofu) with your Week 2 shop. Buy the Week 2 whole chicken the day before you plan to break it down — it must be fresh, not frozen, and fresh chicken has a short window.

## 🥩 Proteins

- [ ] Boneless, skinless chicken breasts — 1 lb (Week 1 Tue, for soup)
- [ ] Large shrimp (16/20 count), peeled and deveined — 1 lb (Week 1 Wed scampi)

- [ ] Bone-in, skin-on chicken thighs — 6 pieces (Week 2 Tue + Friday leftovers)

- [ ] Shrimp (16/20 count) OR firm tofu — 8 oz (Week 2 Mon Pad Thai protein)

## 📝 Notes

**French onion soup (Week 1 Sat) needs a huge amount of onions.** 5–6 large yellow onions is not too many — they cook down to about 10% of their raw volume over 45–60 minutes. Don't rush the caramelization.

**Pad Thai (Week 2 Mon):** Soak rice noodles in cold water for 30 minutes before cooking — don't boil them. They finish in the wok. Mise en place is even more critical here than in the stir-fry.

**Stir-fry (Week 1 Fri):** Dry sherry is an acceptable sub for Shaoxing wine. If you're buying it anyway for French onion soup, you're covered — just reserve some before the onion soup.

**Whole chicken (Week 2 Wed):** Watch the Serious Eats chicken breakdown video before you start. Takes about 20 minutes the first time; 8 minutes by Week 6.
