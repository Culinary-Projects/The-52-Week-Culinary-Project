## Key Curing Ratios

| Cure Type | Salt | Sugar | Time | Notes |
|-----------|------|-------|------|-------|
| Gravlax (standard) | 2 parts | 1 part | 48–72 hrs | Dill, white pepper |
| Candied salmon | 1 part | 1 part | 24–36 hrs | Brown sugar, black pepper |
| Pancetta / dry cure | ~2.5% of meat weight | Small amount | 5–7 days | Black pepper, aromatics |
| Quick pickle brine | 1:1 water/vinegar | 1 tbsp per cup | 1–2 hrs | Salt, spice to taste |

## Key Sausage Notes

- Fat content: 25–30% for juicy sausage; less → dry
- Keep everything cold: meat, grinder parts, bowl
- Test cook a small patty before stuffing — adjust seasoning before casings
- Sheep casings (for merguez) available at good butchers or online
- No casings? Form as patties or loose ground
