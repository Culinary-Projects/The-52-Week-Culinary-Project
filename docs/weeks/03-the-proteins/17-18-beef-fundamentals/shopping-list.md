# 🛒 Shopping List — Weeks 17–18: Beef Fundamentals

← [Back to Full Guide](index.md) | [Back to Quick Ref](quick-ref.md)

---

> **How to use this list:** Week 15 is all about precision and high heat. Week 16 is braises — patient and hands-off. Buy your Week 15 ribeye on Thursday so you can dry brine it Friday night for Monday. The standing rib roast (Saturday Week 15) needs to be ordered from a butcher if you're not in a good-stocked area. The brisket (Wednesday Week 16) needs to start by 11am if you're eating dinner.

---

## ⚠️ Equipment Check

- [ ] **Instant-read thermometer** — non-negotiable this block. You are cooking to temperature, not to time. Under $30 for a Thermapen-style; Lavatools or ThermoPop work great.
- [ ] **Cast iron skillet** — essential for Mon/Tue/Fri steaks. The heavier the better.
- [ ] **Large Dutch oven** — needed for all three braises (Mon/Tue/Wed, Week 16). 5–7 quart.
- [ ] **Wire rack** — for the reverse sear setup (steak on rack in a sheet pan in the oven)

---

## 🥩 Proteins — Week 15

- [ ] **Ribeye steak** — 1.5–2 inch thick; bone-in or boneless (Mon dry brine/reverse sear). Buy Thursday, dry brine immediately.
- [ ] **Strip steaks** — 2 pieces, 1 inch thick (Tuesday pan sear)
- [ ] **Ground beef** — 80/20, NOT lean; 1.5 lbs (Wednesday smash burgers)
- [ ] **Skirt steak** — 1–1.5 lbs; inside skirt preferred (Friday — marinate overnight)
- [ ] **Standing rib roast (prime rib)** — 2-bone minimum; USDA Choice or Prime. Order ahead from a butcher.

---

## 🥩 Proteins — Week 16

- [ ] **Bone-in beef short ribs** — English cut; 3 lbs (Monday braise)
- [ ] **Chuck roast** — 3 lbs, bone-in preferred (Tuesday pot roast)
- [ ] **Brisket** — flat or point; 3–4 lbs, USDA Choice minimum (Wednesday; start early)

## 🔢 Timing Notes

| Day | Key Timing |
|-----|-----------|
| **Week 15 Thu** | Buy and immediately dry brine the ribeye (uncovered, in fridge) |
| **Week 15 Fri** | Marinate skirt steak overnight |
| **Week 16 Mon** | Short ribs: braise starts at 3 PM, eats at 7 PM |
| **Week 16 Tue** | Chuck: braise in by 3 PM |
| **Week 16 Wed** | Brisket: start by 10–11 AM; it needs 5–6 hours minimum |
