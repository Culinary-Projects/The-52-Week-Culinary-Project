## Key Indian Spice Reference

| Spice | Technique | When to Add | Example Use |
|-------|-----------|-------------|-------------|
| Cumin seeds | Toast whole in fat | Beginning (tarka) | Dal, rice |
| Mustard seeds | Sizzle in fat until popping | Beginning (tarka) | South Indian, dal |
| Turmeric | Add to onion; cook out raw flavor | Early | All curries |
| [Garam masala](https://www.seriouseats.com/garam-masala-recipe) | Ground blend | At end / finish | [Butter chicken, tikka](https://www.seriouseats.com/stovetop-butter-chicken) |
| Amchur (dry mango powder) | Add near end | Finish | [Chana masala](https://www.seriouseats.com/chana-masala-recipe-8665080) |
| Asafoetida (hing) | Tiny pinch, never cook too long | With tarka | [Dal (digestive role)](https://www.seriouseats.com/moong-dal-recipe-8744764) |
