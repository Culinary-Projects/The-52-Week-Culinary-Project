## Week 37 — Hummus, Shakshuka, Shawarma

| Day | Dish | Technique | Key Point |
|-----|------|-----------|-----------|
| Mon | Hummus b'Tahini | Dried chickpeas + baking soda; blend warm | Warm chickpeas → silkier blend |
| Tue | Shakshuka | Tomato-pepper sauce; eggs poached in it | Don't over-set the yolks |
| Wed | Baba Ganoush + Muhammara | Direct flame char; roasted pepper walnut blend | Black eggplant skin = the smoke flavor |
| Thu | **Rest** | — | — |
| Fri | Falafel + Fattoush | Raw soaked chickpeas (never canned); 350°F fry | Must use dried chickpeas |
| Sat | **PROJECT:** Beef Shawarma + Tabbouleh | Thin-cut marinated beef; broil or sear hot | Tabbouleh = mostly parsley |

## Middle Eastern Pantry Essentials

| Ingredient | Role | Source |
|------------|------|--------|
| Tahini | Sesame paste; base for hummus, sauces | Soom or Seed + Mill brand |
| Sumac | Tart red spice; dressings, kebab finish | Middle Eastern market |
| Za'atar | Thyme-based herb blend; used on bread and labneh | Middle Eastern market |
| Pomegranate molasses | Tart-sweet syrup; fattoush + glazes | Middle Eastern market |
| Dried black lime (loomi) | Intensely citrusy; used whole in stews | Middle Eastern market |
| Rose water | Floral; desserts + kabsa | Middle Eastern market |
| Harissa | North African chile paste | Available widely |
| Labneh | Strained yogurt; smooth, tart | Make by straining Greek yogurt overnight |

## Key Ratios

| Preparation | Ratio |
|-------------|-------|
| Hummus tahini | 1 cup cooked chickpeas : ¼ cup tahini : 2 tbsp lemon : 1 garlic clove |
| Tabbouleh | By volume: ¾ parsley : ¼ everything else (not the reverse) |
| Tahini sauce | 1 part tahini : 1 part lemon juice : garlic + water to thin |
| Falafel | Soak dried chickpeas 12–24 hrs; do not cook; grind raw |
