# Recipe Standards

Use these criteria when selecting recipes.

## Must have

- Teaches a clear technique or skill.
- Fits the time window (30-45 min weeknights, longer on Saturday).
- Uses common equipment and home-friendly steps.
- Includes clear doneness or texture cues.

## Prefer

- Reuses ingredients across the week.
- Includes a simple side or component to practice.
- Offers variations or scaling notes.

## Avoid

- Unreliable or vague instructions.
- Ingredient lists with hard-to-find items (unless planned).
- Recipes that are mostly assembly with little technique.
